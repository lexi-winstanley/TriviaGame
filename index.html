Woodworking Trivia

This trivia game is all about woodworking! Once you click the button below and the game begins a question will appear on screen and you will be given a certain amount of time in which to answer. After you answer or time runs out, the correct answer will be shown and a new question will load. Your timer will reset each time a new question appears. Once you have gone through all the questions a screen will display the total you got correct/incorrect.
